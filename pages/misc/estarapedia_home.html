Welcome to Estarapedia, the definitive compendium of knowledge meticulously curated by the esteemed Ephelion Company's Vision Commission.


The Estarapedia stands as the cornerstone of knowledge on Estara. With a commitment to preserving the rich tapestry of Estara's history, culture, and lore, the Estarapedia serves as an invaluable resource for scholars. Through extensive research and dedication to accuracy, our mission is to illuminate the depths of Estara's past, present, and future. Whether sifting through the annals of ancient kingdoms, unraveling arcane mysteries, or charting the rise and fall of noble houses, the Ephelion Company stands as a beacon of enlightenment, guiding all who seek to uncover the secrets of Estara and beyond.
