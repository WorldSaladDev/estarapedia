Item Statistics

Accretion Flask

Equipment (magic container)

Spell Capacity: 5 slots

Description

An accretion flask is a device designed to slowly accumulate magical energy from the ambient field. The Ephelion Company's proprietary design is the most widespread due to its relative afforability. It consists of a metal (usually Eingahr) flask filled with Draihar oil.
