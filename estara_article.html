Estara is the closest planet to the star Rey. Estara supports life and harbors intelligent civilization.

Estara has a mass of 3.36332*10^24kg and a radius of 4783km. It has a total land area of 48,882,154km^2, with land making up 17% of the planet's surface.

Geography

Estara has four continental landmasses. Estaran maps typically center on the Mouth of Estara, a large undersea basin of unknown origin.

The Mouth of Estara

Main article:

Storms swirl constantly in the skies above and around the Mouth, deterring sailors from traveling through these waters. The crater's eastern edge is lined with islands, the largest of which are known as the Pit Isles. The islands are surrounded in significant fields of chaotic Draihar Being situated on the edge of the Mouth of Estara, the Pit Isles are also subject to near-constant bad weather, with high winds and heavy rain.

The Pit Isles are not known to have a permanent population due to the hazardous conditions, but researchers and thrillseekers occasionally brave the shores of these lands, usually without returning.

Ekhala

Main article:

To the east of the Mouth lies Ekhala, the largest of Estara’s continents. The continent is split by the Kaylik mountain range, of which the Thorn of Estara – the tallest mountain on Estara – is part. The bay of Temchael is surrounded by mountains in the eastern part of the continent. The Dunes of Ekhala spans from the Kayliks to the western edge of the continent. North of the dunes is the Berreth Steppe, a plain of dry but fertile land, and north of the steppe lies the Umbreweald, an icy forest teeming with magical beasts. A series of peninsulae along the southern edge of the continent are dotted with large coastal cities . The Altueran island chain trails off from the southeastern edge of Ekhala.

Climate conditions vary across Ekhala. The Dunes see very little rainfall each year and receive direct sunlight on most days. The Berreth Steppe sports cool winds unhindered by trees or mountains, accompanied by light rainfall. Further north, the pine forests of the Umbreweald freeze over during the harsher winters. The peninsulae of Hamshi enjoy salty sea spray and gentle winds that cool the landscape. In Temchael, the weather is cool, calm and temperate year round. The Altuerans are known for their warm and humid climate.

Many settlements are spread across Ekhala, with a number of great cities. Three nations call the mainland home: Temchael in their namesake bay; the Hamshi, with their population primarily centered on the southern peninsulae; and the Tir Moran, who inhabit the northern reaches of the Berreth Steppe and the Umbreweald. The Altueran Islands are also home to a number of small nations who have a shared culture.

Tzizen

Main article: Tzizen

Tzizen, the smallest continent, can be found northwest of the Mouth of Estara. Most of its land and population lies in the Misty Valley, a valley between the two branches of the Tzizen Mountain range. On the northern end of the continent sits Da Yonjiang, Estara's largest active volcano. In the sea to the west, the sparsely inhabited Wa Tzizen islands protect the mainland from the storms of the Great Sea. North of the mainland lies the Yonjiang Islands, a volcanic island chain originating from the Yonjiang hotspot.

The mainland of Tzizen is primarily composed of cloud forests, with cold temperatures and high humidity, but rarely extreme weather conditions. The southern tip of the continent is covered in marshland as a result of the many rivers flowing down from the mountain and down the valley. Further north, the cloud forests give way to boreal woods. The Yonjiang Islands are barren, cold and incredibly windy. The Wa Tzizen islands are characterized by lowland jungles and frequent storms

The continent and surrounding islands are the domain of the Kingdom of Tzizen. The majority of the population live within the Misty Valley. A few settlements are established on the Wa Tzizen Islands, and the Yonjiang Islands are a penal colony with an autonomous local government.

Anka

Main article:

South of Tzizen, across the Strait of Fluire, is the continent Anka. Anka's landscape is dominated by the Vacia desert, Estara's second largest desert. The Cubroda mountains line the northern and western edges of the continent. To the east and across the Bay of Vione towers the lone mountain Volare. The islands Regana and Fiori are situated west of the sea of Prima. To the south, the Melar Forest spans across the Isthmus of Melar and into Estica.

The Vacia desert is dry and warm year round, with little rainfall. However, life flourishes along the rivers that run through the desert. The Melar forest recieves heavy rainfall, amplified by the tall canopies that capture low-flying clouds. The eastern edge of the continent is composed mostly of sparse forests and grasslands with moderate rainfall. The islands of Regana and Fiori are known for their fair and consistent weather.

Anka is one of the most heavily populated continents. Vast cities cover the western coast, and a number of distinct cultures flourish in the variety of resources offered by the continent. The Primetira Kingdom holds domain over all of Anka and Estica, except Regana and Fiori, where the independent nation of Regana e Fiori resides.

Estica

Main article:

Estica lies south of Anka. The Melar Forest thins out as it spreads south of the isthmus, giving way to grasslands and finally the Ala Via mountain range, named for its highest peak. The Otoros Islands fill the sea east of the mainland.

South of the Melar Forest, Estica is composed primarily of grasslands, with moderate rain and fair temperatures that drop farther south. The continent extends into polar regions at its southern tip. The Otoros islands recieve heavy rain, and are covered in dense jungle.

Estica is less populated than Anka, but the capital city of the Primetira Kingdom - also the largest city in Estara - is situated at the base of Ala Via. Numerous other settlements are spread across the continent. The Otoros islands are known to be inhabited by primitive peoples. Little effort has been made to contact them.

Culture and Politics

There are a number of cultures inhabiting Estara.

Hamshi

Main article:

The Hamshi nation occupies the southern portion of Ekhala up to the Breac River. They primarily speak the Hamshi language.

Timekeeping

One Estaran day is equal to 24 hours. An Estaran year last 240 days. The calendar is split into ten months, each with three weeks lasting eight days.

In many cultures across Estara, it is believed that one transitions to adulthood at the age of twenty-four years. The number twenty-four is of special interest because of its connection with the cycles of Estara and its moons.

The last nation to join in adhering to this standard was Hamshi, in the sixty-second Year Estaran (Y.E.). 1YE falls on the year the Estaran Calendar was devised by the Primetira Kingdom.

Moons

Estara has two orbiting moons.

Ephelion

Main article:

Ephelion is the closer of Estara's two moons. It has a rocky surface and a dark, slightly purple appearance. It has an angular size of approximately 0.555 degrees when viewed from Estara. Ephelion orbits Estara once every 8 days. The second from last month of the Estaran calendar is named after Ephelion, and the 8 day weeks are based on Ephelion's orbital period.

Acies

Main article:

Acies is the further of Estara's two moons. It has a rocky surface with a light reddish appearance. It is significantly larger than Ephelion, but is further away from Estara, thus it has an angular size of approximately 0.3 degrees when viewed from Estara. Acies orbits Estara once every 24 days. The second month of the Estaran calendar is named after Acies, and the 24 day months are based on Acies' orbital period.
